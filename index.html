📋 Cash Payment Instructions

  1. Note your Fine ID shown above.
  2. Visit the Hostel Office during working hours (10 AM – 5 PM).
  3. Pay the exact amount in cash.
  4. The warden will mark your fine as paid in the system.
  5. You will receive an on-screen confirmation once updated.
